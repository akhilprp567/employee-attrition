Employee Attrition AI

An intelligent platform that uses Machine Learning to predict, understand, and prevent employee attrition — empowering HR teams to make proactive, data-driven decisions.

AI Attrition Prediction
ML model trained on real workforce data delivers accurate attrition risk probabilities for every employee.
Risk Classification
Employees are automatically classified into Low, Medium, and High risk tiers with detailed reasoning.
Interactive Dashboard
Rich visualizations — donut charts, radar charts, area fills, and animated KPI cards — all live.
Data-Driven Insights
HR teams get actionable, ML-backed insights on salary, satisfaction, commute, and work-life balance factors.
How It Works
1
Add Employee Data
Enter employee details — age, salary, satisfaction scores, work experience, distance from home, and more.
2
ML Model Processes
Our trained Random Forest model evaluates the inputs and computes an attrition probability score in real time.
3
Risk Score & Reason
Each employee receives a risk score (0–100%) and a human-readable AI-generated reason explaining the prediction.
4
HR Takes Action
HR managers use the insights to intervene early — improving retention and reducing costly attrition.
